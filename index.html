About me?

I am a junior Computer Science and Mathematics major at Harvey Mudd College, a small liberal arts college outside of Los Angeles. I started coding in college and have loved it ever since. I'm still trying to navigate Computer Science and where I see myself headed, but some of my interests lie in data science, artificial intelligence, web development, and mobile development.

I also really enjoy learning about topics outside of Computer Science, such as history, languages, and social issues. At Harvey Mudd College, I am concentrating in Asian Studies to learn more about the continent that my ancestors came from and it has been a great experience. I am also involved in various organizations on campus, such as ACM-W, a women in tech group that aims to create a welcoming environment for women to learn more about CS.
